Our Story

Born from a Love of Coffee & Community

Ora Café was created to bring together exceptional coffee, great food, and meaningful moments in a beautiful, welcoming space. Inspired by modern café culture, we offer specialty coffee, handcrafted dishes, and a calm contemporary atmosphere where people can connect, work, and unwind.

From our sculpted interiors and warm lighting to our thoughtfully curated menu, every element of Ora has been designed to create an experience that feels both inspiring and comfortable with every visit.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, Ora Café accepts reservations. You can book through our website reservation form or contact us directly. Walk-ins are also welcome depending on availability, especially during off-peak hours.

Ora Café offers an inclusive menu with a wide selection of dishes suitable for guests with Halal dietary requirements. While not officially Halal-certified, we take dietary needs seriously. Please speak with our team before ordering to confirm specific dishes.

We're open Monday to Friday from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM, and Saturday to Sunday from 8:00 AM to 11:00 PM. Hours may vary slightly on public holidays — we recommend calling ahead.

Yes! Ora Café features both a beautiful indoor dining space with sculpted interiors and warm lighting, as well as an outdoor terrace seating area — perfect for enjoying the Nairobi weather.

Yes, parking is available at our Waiyaki Way location. There is both on-site and nearby parking depending on peak hours. We recommend arriving a few minutes early during busy periods.
